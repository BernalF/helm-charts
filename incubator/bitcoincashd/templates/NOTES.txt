bitcoincashd RPC can be accessed via port {{ .Values.service.rpcPort }} on the following DNS name from within your cluster:
{{ template "bitcoincashd.fullname" . }}.{{ .Release.Namespace }}.svc.cluster.local

To connect to bitcoincashd RPC:

1. Forward the port for the node:

  kubectl port-forward --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} $(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l app={{ template "bitcoincashd.name" . }} -o jsonpath='{ .items[0].metadata.name }') {{ .Values.service.rpcPort }}

2. Connect using the bitcoincashd cli, then provide your password:
  $ curl --user rpcuser:rpcpassword -k http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.service.rpcPort }} --data-binary '{"jsonrpc": "1.0", "id":"curltest", "method": "getinfo", "params": [] }' -H 'content-type: text/plain;'
